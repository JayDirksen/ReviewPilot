Common questions
A few things hosts ask us.
Does this violate Airbnb's rules?
ReviewPilot sends messages directly to your guest via SMS or email — outside Airbnb's platform entirely. There's nothing in Airbnb's terms that prevents you from following up with a guest after their stay.
What if I had a difficult guest I don't want to ask?
Every checkout triggers a one-tap notification to you first. Tap skip and nothing gets sent. You're always in control of who receives a message.
I already use Hospitable or a PMS — do I need this?
Most PMS tools bury the review request inside a general checkout message. ReviewPilot sends a dedicated, perfectly timed message focused only on the review — which converts significantly better.
How do you get my guest's contact info?
You simply enter the guest's email or phone number after each checkout — it takes 10 seconds. We're working on direct integrations to make this fully automatic.
